# Intangiriro

**_Kin_** ni ururimi rwa porogaramu rwakozwe hagamijwe gufasha abantu bavuga Ikinyarwanda kwiga gukora porogaramu mu buryo bworoshye bwo kubareka kwandika porogaramu za mudasobwa mu rurimi rwabo kavukire Ikinyarwanda.

> Kin yagenewe gufasha umuntu kwandika porogaramu mu Kinyarwanda, ururimi kavukire rw’Abanyarwanda.  
> Intego nyamukuru ni ugufasha abantu gutangira kwiga gukora porogaramu mu buryo bworoshye. Nubwo ikwiye cyane mu kwigisha no kwiga porogaramu, ntabwo iremezwa nk’iyabugenewe ikoreshwa mu bikorwa by’umwuga cyangwa mu nganda.

## Kuki?

Abantu batazi Icyongereza baba bafite imbogamizi yo gutangira kwiga gukora porogaramu.  
Byongeye kandi, indimi nyinshi za porogaramu zigira ibice byinshi bisaba kwandika ibintu byinshi (boilerplate code) bigoye kumva ku bantu batangiye.  
N’ubwo hari indimi nko **JavaScript** zidafite ibyo bice byinshi, usanga ziri mu buryo bugoye kandi bunini butorohereza umunyeshuri ushaka kwiga neza.  

**_Kin_** rero yaje gukemura ibyo bibazo, ikuraho inzitizi z’ururimi, igahitamo gusa ibice by’ingenzi byagufasha gutangira neza.

## Ushobora Kugera He na Kin?

**_Kin_** iguha ubushobozi bwo gukoresha urutonde rw'ibintu (arrays) n'ibyegeranyo (objects) mu buryo bworoshye, hamwe n’imvugo zigenzura ibikorwa (control statements) n’imyitwarire y’isubiramo (loops), ndetse n’imvugo z’inyongeramusaruro n’inyongeramajwi (Input/Output).  
Ifite uburyo bworoshye bwo gukora imirimo isanzwe nko gufata no gufungura amadosiye (file handling), kandi ishyigikira ubwoko butandukanye bw’amakuru (data types), bigatuma ikora ibintu byinshi bitandukanye.  
Ikiyongera nk’ubushobozi bwo gukora amategeko y’ikoranabuhanga (system commands) bituma Kin iba ifite urwego rwo hejuru rwo gukora ibintu bigoye ku bashaka kugera kure mu mikorere yayo.
