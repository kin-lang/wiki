# Intangiriro

**_Kin_** ni ururimi rwa porogaramu rwahanzwe hagamijwe gufasha abavuga Ikinyarwanda kwiga porogaramu mu buryo bworoshye, babikora mu rurimi rwabo kavukire Ikinyarwanda.

> Kin yashyizweho kugira ngo umuntu abashe kwandika porogaramu mu Kinyarwanda, ururimi kavukire rw’Abanyarwanda.  
> Intego yayo ni ukorohereza abantu kwinjira mu masomo ya porogaramu. Nubwo ari ururimi rukwiriye cyane mu kwigisha cyangwa kwiga, ntihizewe ko rukoreshwa mu rwego rw’umwuga cyangwa mu buryo bwo kubyaza umusaruro.

## Kuki?

Abatagira ubumenyi mu Cyongereza ntibashobora gutangira kwiga porogaramu.  
Byongeye kandi, indimi nyinshi za porogaramu zigira **boilerplate code** (imvugo z’igenamiterere zitavogerwa) bigatuma abantu bashya batabasha gutangira mu buryo bworoshye.  
Uretse ibyo, n’iyo ubonye ururimi rutagira izo **boilerplate code** nka `JavaScript`, usanga rufite urusobe runini rw’ibintu bigoye bitarorohereza umuntu ushaka kwiga.

Muri `Kin`, ibyo bibazo byarakosowe aho nta mbogamizi z’ururimi zihari, kandi hatoranyijwe gusa ibintu by’ingenzi umuntu akeneye mu myigire ye.

## Ushobora Kugeza He na Kin?

**_Kin_** igufasha mu buryo bworoshye mu gukoresha **array** na **object**, igatanga uburyo butandukanye bwo kugenzura gahunda (**control statements**) no gukoresha imizunguruko (**loop statements**), uburyo bwo kwakira no gusohora amakuru (**Input/Output**), ndetse n’ubworoherane bwo gukora imirimo yihariye (**functions**) mu guhuza no gutunganya code.

Ifite uburyo bwubatswemo bwo gufata no kwandika ku nyandiko (**file handling**) ndetse n’imikorere yorohereza imirimo isanzwe. Ikindi, ishyigikira ubwoko butandukanye bw’amakuru (**data types**) bikayigira indimi ikora ibintu byinshi.  
Ibyo byose byiyongera ku bindi bigezweho nka gahunda zishobora gutuma ukora amategeko ya sisiteme (**system commands**), bigatanga urwego rwo hejuru ku bantu bashaka gukora ibintu bigoye kurushaho.
